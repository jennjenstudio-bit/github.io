About ME

Lynn Jenn is a freelance sound designer, music producer, and songwriter whose work explores the boundaries of what can be expressed through music. She integrates contemporary styles with elements of Malaysian culture to shape a distinctive and adaptable sound.

Based in Kuala Lumpur, she specializes in sound design for visual media and games, while also working across music production and songwriting. She creates music and sound for visual media and collaborative projects, and also performs as a pianist.

Original Composition

Come Back Home

Composer · Lyricist

"Come Back Home" is a song born from the shared experience of being far from the place we call home. Whether we've left for education, work, or to chase a dream, many of us live away from the familiar comfort of our families, friends, food, and culture.

"Come Back Home" is a reminder that no matter how long we’re gone, how high we climb, or how far we go, there will always be a place for us to return to. A place that waits with open arms. A place we’ll always call home.

OHEYA

Composer · Lyricist

"Oheya" is a vibrant celebration of Malaysia’s food culture, sung in multiple languages to reflect the country’s diverse voices and traditions.

Playful and inviting, the song brings together the many flavours that define Malaysia—drawing from Malay, Chinese, Indian, Nyonya, and countless other culinary influences.

“Oheya” is more than just a song—it’s an invitation to experience Malaysia through its flavours, where every dish has a story, every meal carries memory, and there’s always room for one more at the table.
